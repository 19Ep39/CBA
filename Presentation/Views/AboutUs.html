Who we are

Our highly skilled and experienced Executive team manage the daily operations of Colonial First State, as well as deliver on established policies and strategies. Meet our independent Trustee and Responsible Entity Board.

Rebecca Ferguson

Executive General Manager, Colonial First State

Rebecca joined Colonial First State (CFS) in March 2010 and was promoted to Executive General Manager for CFS in August 2012. Prior to this Rebecca was the General Manager, Marketing and Distribution at CFS, responsible for marketing and distribution activity across the CFS business which includes retail investments, platforms and corporate super.

Tom Cruise

General Manager, Product and Investments

Tom first joined Colonial First State in 2002. As General Manager, Product & Investments, Tom is responsible for managing and driving the development of Colonial First State's products (including the FirstChoice and FirstWrap platforms), investment functions including the FirstChoice multi-manager investment team, Third Party Alliance funds, and the Performance and Analytics team.

Jeremy Renner

General Manager, Marketing
